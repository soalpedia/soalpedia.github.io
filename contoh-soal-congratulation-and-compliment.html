4. what is different between congratulation and compliment?


To compliment is saying something positive about someone, even if small or trivial. You look great! You smell nice.

To congratulate is to recognize an event that carries importance for someone. Congratulations with your birthday, degree, baby etc etc.

1. compliment

2. compliment

3. congratulations

Jawaban:

The correct answers are:

1. Hi Andre, nice car you got there! (compliment)

2. What a beautiful garden you have, Mr. Smith! (compliment)

3. Hi Alif, I heard you have been promoted, congratulations man! (congratulation)

In the given sentences:

- Sentence 1 is a compliment because it expresses admiration for Andre's car.

- Sentence 2 is a compliment because it expresses admiration for Mr. Smith's beautiful garden.

- Sentence 3 is a congratulation because it offers congratulations to Alif for being promoted.

1. Intention

Velma: What are you doing Fio?

Fio: Nothing. I am just playing a game.

Velma: Have you heard that Amy house was on fire?

Fio: Yes, I have.

Velma: Don’t you want to help him? I thought you two quite close.

Fio: I intended to help him, but he said that he is fine and he doesn’t need my help.

Velma: He really said that?

Fio: Yes, he did.

Velma: Maybe he got emotional and said that to you. Just let him alone for now.

Fio: Yes I know.

2. Congratulation

Mimin: ”Who won the football match yesterday?”

Udin : ”Our team did. We won two to one.”

Dayu: ”Congratulation. I hope you guys will be better next year.”

Udin: ”Thank you.”

3. Compliment

Rina: Dion! I think I saw your father yesterday.

Dion: Did you see my father in the airport yesterday?

Rina: Yes, Dion. He wore a very nice leather jacket. I like it!

Dion: I bought it for him. He looks great with that.

Rina: Yes I agree Dion.
