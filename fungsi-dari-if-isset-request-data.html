Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

4! + 3! =

= (4 × 3 × 2 × 1) + (3 × 2 × 1)

= (12 × 2 × 1) + (6 × 1)

= (24 × 1) + 6

= 24 + 6

= 30

4! + 3! =

Faktorial

[tex]N! = N \times (N - 1) \times (N - 2).......N[/tex]
