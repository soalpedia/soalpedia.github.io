5. write down the generic structure of the text learning english​


Jawaban:

Jenis-jenis teks dalam bahasa Inggris

1. Descriptive Text

Tujuan:

Menjelaskan kepada pembaca mengenai seseorang, tempat, atau benda secara detail.

Struktur:

Identification (pengenalan)

Description (deskripsi)

Tenses yang digunakan di dalam descriptive text Simple Present tense

2. Recount Text

Tujuan:

Menceritakan kepada pembaca mengenai kejadian yang sudah terjadi di masa lampau

Struktur:

Orientation (pengenalan)

Event (peristiwa

Reorientation (kesimpulan)

Di dalam recount text, tenses yang digunakan adalah past tense

3. Narrative Text

Tujuan:

Untuk menghibur pembaca dan untuk menceritakan cerita atau kisah

Struktur:

Orientation (pengenalan)

Complication (konflik)

Resolution (pemecahan masalah)

Reorientation (kesimpulan)

Tenses yang digunakan di dalam narrative text adalah past tense. Narrative dan recount text memang hampir mirip. Keduanya sama-sama menceritakan kejadian di masa lampau dan sama-sama

menggunakan past tense (simple past tense, simple past continuous tense, atau past perfect tense. Yang membedakan adalah narrative text berupa dongeng, legenda, folklore sedangkan recount text biasanya berupa biography, atau untuk menceritakan pengalaman pribadi.

Hal lain yang membedakan antara narrative dan recount text adalah struktur teksnya. Di dalam narrative text menceritakan konflik yang terjadi sedangkan di recount text tidak ada konflik yang terjadi.

10. write generic structure (text structure) of news item text!


Generic structures of News Item text :

Main Event / Newsworthy EventBackground Event / ElaborationSource

Pembahasan

News item is a kind of text that has a purpose to inform readers / listeners about events of the day which are considered newsworthy or important.

Generic structures :

A. Main event / newsworthy event

=> It is the main information. It explains the summary of an important event happens.

B. Background event / elaboration

=> It explains the the background of an event in details. Who are involved, where does it happen and how does it happen.

C. Source

=> It consists of the source of the news in the form of interviewees, the witnesses' comment, the expert's opinion, and the statement of involved subjects in an event.

19. generic structure itu apa?


Generic structure adalah sistematika pada suatu alur yang tersaji di dalam sebuah teks. Anda juga bisa mengatakan generic structure sebagai tahapan demi tahapan pemetaan gagasan atau informasi di dalam teks. Hal ini identik sekali dengan plot.

in English
Generic structure is a system of a plot in a text. Or else generic structure can be described as the steps to map the idea or information in the text. It is really identical with the plot.

30. find out 13 kind texts in english by using the purpose,the generic structure, and the language feature Please master/teaching assitant


Jawaban:

Sure, I can help you with that. Here are 13 kinds of texts in English including their purposes, generic structures, and language features:

1. Narrative

- Purpose: To tell a story or describe a series of events

- Generic Structure: Orientation, Complication, Resolution, and Coda

- Language Features: Past tense, chronological order, dialogues, and descriptive language

2. Descriptive

- Purpose: To describe a person, place, or thing

- Generic Structure: Identification, Description, and Interpretation

- Language Features: Adjectives, sensory language, figurative language, and present tense

3. Expository

- Purpose: To explain a topic or idea

- Generic Structure: Introduction, Body, and Conclusion

- Language Features: Facts, statistics, examples, cause-and-effect relationships, and objective tone

4. Argumentative

- Purpose: To persuade the reader to agree with a viewpoint or position

- Generic Structure: Introduction, Thesis, Arguments, and Conclusion

- Language Features: Logical reasoning, evidence, counterarguments, persuasive tone, and rhetorical questions

5. Instructional

- Purpose: To give directions or explain how to do something

- Generic Structure: Goal, Material/Ingredients, Steps, and Conclusion

- Language Features: Imperative verbs, simple present tense, diagrams or illustrations, and sequential language

6. Persuasive

- Purpose: To convince the reader to take a specific action or adopt a particular viewpoint

- Generic Structure: Introduction, Body, and Conclusion

- Language Features: Emotive language, rhetorical devices, repetition, persuasive arguments, and persuasive tone

7. Informative

- Purpose: To provide information about a topic or issue

- Generic Structure: Introduction, Background Information, Main Point, Conclusion

- Language Features: Explanatory language, statistics, factual information, simple present tense, and objective tone

8. Comparison and Contrast

- Purpose: To show the differences and similarities between two or more things

- Generic Structure: Introduction, Criteria, Body, and Conclusion

- Language Features: Comparative and contrastive language, adjectives, and adverbs

9. Review

- Purpose: To provide a critical evaluation of a product, service, or event

- Generic Structure: Introduction, Summary, Analysis, and Recommendation

- Language Features: Descriptive language, evaluative language, personal opinion, and objective tone

10. Interview

- Purpose: To gather information through a question-and-answer format

- Generic Structure: Introduction, Questions, and Responses

- Language Features: Direct and indirect speech, present tense, and appropriate question format

11. Advertisement

- Purpose: To persuade the reader to buy a product or service

- Generic Structure: Attention grabber, Introduction, Product Information, Benefits, Call to Action

- Language Features: Superlative language, persuasive language, catchy phrases, and product features

12. Formal Letter or Email

- Purpose: To communicate in a professional or business context

- Generic Structure: Introduction, Body, and Closing

- Language Features: Formal language, appropriate salutation and closing, clear message, and respectful tone

13. Personal Letter or Email

- Purpose: To communicate in a personal or informal context

- Generic Structure: Greeting, Body, and Closing

- Language Features: Informal language, personal voice, conversational tone, and casual structure

37. tolong jelaskan structure b inggris dengan singkat, padat dan jelas bagi yang ingin menjawab pertanyaan ini semoga diberi kemudahan dalam belajar


Nouns

Adalah kata benda didalam sebuah kalimat. Jika kita menuliskan nama seseorang pada sebuah kalimat seperti “Tono” maka “Tono” adalah kata bendanya. Nouns atau kata benda bisa saja We, They, I, You, She, He, It. Didalam nouns dibagi menjadi 5 bagian yaitu Proper Nouns, Abstract Nouns, Concrete Nouns, Singular & Plural Nouns, Countable and Uncountable Nouns

Pronouns

Adalah kata ganti untuk seseorang, misalkan “They Love Me” They akan berfungsi sebagai subject pada sebuah kalimat, “I Love Them” Them adalah kata ganti dari object “They”.

Didalam pronouns ada beberapa bagian diantaranya reflexive Pronoun, Indefinite Pronoun, Possessive Pronoun, Relative Pronoun, Demostrative Pronoun, Expletive it And There, Personal Pronoun.

Verb

Adalah kata kerja dalam sebuah kalimat. Misalkan i drew a paper. “drew” adalah kata kerja yang berarti menggambar ( masa lampau).

Adjective

Adalah kata sifat. Contohnya beautiful, handsome, smart, dan lain sebagainya.

Prepositions

Adalah kata depan dalam sebuah kalimat. Contohnya in, at, on.

Adverbs

Adalah kata keterangan yang terdapat dalam sebuah kalimat. Contohnya in the room “room” adalah keterangan tempat.

Conjunction

Adalah kata penghubung pada sebuah kalimat. Contohnya and, or, but, dll.
