11. there are five relative pronouns in english construct your sentence each pronoun? ​


Jawaban:

There are five relative pronouns in English construct your sentence each pronoun. There are

1. Who

2. Whom

3. Whose

4. Which

5. That

Penjelasan:

1. Who is a subject pronoun, which means it can be used as the subject of a sentence or clause.

2. Whom is an object pronoun, which means it describes a person on the receiving end of an action.

3. Whose is a possessive pronoun. It gives more information about a person by describing things that belong to them.

4. Which and that are relative pronouns that apply to objects. Which in relative clauses that aren’t crucial to a sentence. Key clauses tend to use that.

1. Kalau secara grafik gini caranya :

contoh : x²+y² = 9 <= r² = 9

c. Bikin sebuah garis vertikal (kalau yang dipilih 2 titik y yang berbeda), atau sebuah garis horizontal (kalau yang dipilih 2 titik x berbeda)

20. construct a rhombus of diagonal 4cm and 6cm ​


☘️ math answer from zheyma Chelsea ☘️

Jawaban:

Draw a line segment say AC = 4 cm.

With A as centre draw an arc of length equal to half of 6cm ie., 3 cm below AC.

With C as centre draw another arc with same radius such that it intersects the previous arc.

Join points A, B and points B, C.

Repeat the same process on other side of AC.
