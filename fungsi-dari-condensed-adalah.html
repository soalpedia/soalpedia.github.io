5. TULIS JAWABAN DENGAN HURUF CAPITAL/BESAR SEMUA ! How to make INDONESIAN KOPI TELU 1. ...... honey, vanilla extract and the egg yolk in a glass.  2. .......boiling water in the glass 3. ...... condensed milk into the glass and mixture them. 4. ......... brewed coffee into the center of the condensed milk.  5. INDONESIAN KOPI TELUR is ready to ....... * ​


Jawaban:

Pertanyaan diatas merupakan procedure text. Procedure text adalah sebuah teks yang memberikan perintah atau langkah-langkah melakukan sesuatu.

How To Make Indonesian Kopi Telur

First, put honey, vanilla extract and the egg yolk in a glass.Then, pour boiling water in the glass.Next, pour condensed milk into the glass and mix them.Last, pour brewed coffee into the center of the condensed milk.Indonesian Kopi Telur is ready to serve.

Pembahasan:

Procedure text adalah jenis text untuk menjelaskan atau memberi instruksi tentang bagaimana melakukan sesuatu hingga selesai. Biasanya ditandai dengan kalimat "How to..." atau "bagaimana..". Pada text di atas dapat kita temukan kalimat "how to make Indonesian Kopi Telur". Maka teks di atas merupakan procedure text.

Ciri-ciri Procedure Text:

Menggunakan present simpleMenggunakan kalimat "How to..."Menggunakan kata kerja (boil, pour, dsb)Menggunakan konjungsi temporal (first, then, next, last)Menggunakan keterangan waktu

37. Here are the action or the steps to iced fruit cocktail with condensed milk es teler


Jawaban:

Fruit Ice with Sweetened Condensed Milk

•150g sugar (1 1/2 cup).

•2 pandan leaves, grated •lengthwise and tied into dead knots.

•3 1/2 tablespoons (50 ml) water.

•400g cooked avocado, diced 1 cm.

•400g ripe jackfruit, seeded and diced into 1cm.

•3 young coconuts, the flesh is peeled.

•The crushed ice cube beam.

•Sweetened condensed milk.

•To make syrup, mix sugar, pandan leaves and water in a small saucepan and heat the mixture for several minutes over low heat until the sugar has dissolved.

•Refrigerate the syrup.

•To serve this dessert, put a spoonful of avocado, jackfruit and young coconut in a serving bowl.

•Add a little syrup to sweeten, top with shaved ice and pour a little sweetened condensed milk.

•Serve immediately.
