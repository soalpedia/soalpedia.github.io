10. Berapa jumlah unsur yang bisa menggunakan Accurate?​


Jawaban:

Accurate adalah perangkat lunak akuntansi yang digunakan oleh banyak bisnis untuk mengelola keuangan mereka. Jumlah "unsur" atau pengguna yang bisa menggunakan Accurate sebenarnya tergantung pada jenis lisensi yang dibeli oleh perusahaan.

Misalnya, untuk versi desktop dari Accurate, biasanya ada pilihan untuk lisensi tunggal (hanya satu pengguna) atau lisensi multi-user (banyak pengguna). Untuk versi multi-user, jumlah pengguna yang diizinkan akan tergantung pada jumlah lisensi yang dibeli oleh perusahaan.

Sementara itu, untuk versi cloud dari Accurate, biasanya tidak ada batasan jumlah pengguna - Anda bisa menambahkan sebanyak mungkin pengguna sesuai kebutuhan bisnis Anda.

Namun, untuk detail yang lebih spesifik, saya sarankan untuk menghubungi langsung pihak Accurate atau distributor resmi mereka, karena kebijakan dan detail mungkin berbeda tergantung pada versi perangkat lunak dan lokasi Anda.

Penjelasan:

Mohon maaf atas kesalahpahaman sebelumnya. Saya mengerti bahwa Anda ingin mengetahui jumlah unsur dalam konteks kimia. Namun, pertanyaan Anda sebelumnya menyebutkan "Accurate" yang merupakan perangkat lunak akuntansi.

Jika Anda ingin mengetahui jumlah unsur dalam tabel periodik, saat ini ada 118 unsur yang diketahui. Unsur-unsur ini terdiri dari unsur-unsur alami dan sintetis yang telah diidentifikasi dan dikarakterisasi oleh ilmuwan.

17. 1. Net Sales 100.000.000 COGS 89.000.000 Operating Expense 8.000.000 Non Operating Revenue 800.000 Non Operating Expense 1.000.000 Tax Expense 250.000 Net Income After Tax? 2. Gross Profit 4.000.000 COGS 36.000.000 Sales 41.500.000 Sales Return 500.000 Sales Discount? 3. Net Sales 78.000 COGS 60.000 Purchase 24.000 Gross Profit. . . % of COGS 4. Net Sales 150.000.000 Net Income After Tax 2% of Net Sales Tax Expense 500.000 Non Operating Expense 1.200.000 Non Operating Revenue 400.000 Operating Expense 1.800.000 Gross Profit? 5. Gross Profit 12.500 Net Sales 44.000 Beginning Inventory 10.000 Ending Inventory 5.000 Net Purchase? 6. Net Sales 67.000 COGS 11.000 Operating Expense 50.000 Tax Expense 1.000 Net Income After Tax? 7. Sales 120.000 Sales Return 3.000 Sales Discount 1.500 Freight in 700 Freight out 800 Net Sales?


1. Net Sales 100.000.000
COGS 89.000.000
Operating Expense 8.000.000
Non Operating Revenue 800.000
Non Operating Expense 1.000.000
Tax Expense 250.000
Net Income After Tax?
Net Income After Tax = Net Sales - COGS - Operating Expense + Non Operating Revenue - Non Operating Expense - Tax Expense
Net Income After Tax = 100jt - 89jt - 8jt + 800rb - 1jt - 250rb
Net Income After Tax = 2.550.000

2. Gross Profit 4.000.000
COGS 36.000.000
Sales 41.500.000
Sales Return 500.000
Sales Discount?
Gross Profit = Net Sales - COGS
Gross Profit = (Sales - Sales Return - Sales Discount) - COGS
4jt = (41,5jt - 500rb - Sales Discount) - 36jt
4jt = (41jt - Sales Discount) - 36jt
Sales Discount = 41jt - 36jt - 4jt
Sales Discount = 1.000.000

3. Net Sales 78.000
COGS 60.000
Purchase 24.000
Gross Profit. . . % of COGS
Gross Profit = Net Sales - COGS
Gross Profit = 78.000 - 60.000 = 18.000
Gross Profit = 18.000 / 60.000 x 100% = 30% of COGS

4. Net Sales 150.000.000
Net Income After Tax 2% of Net Sales
Tax Expense 500.000
Non Operating Expense 1.200.000
Non Operating Revenue 400.000
Operating Expense 1.800.000
Gross Profit?
Gross Profit = Net Sales - COGS
Gross Profit = 150jt - 0 (TIDAK ADA SALDONYA)
Gross Profit = 150.000.000

5. Gross Profit 12.500
Net Sales 44.000
Beginning Inventory 10.000
Ending Inventory 5.000
Net Purchase?
Net Sales - Gross Profit = COGS
44.000 - 12.500 = 31.500
COGS = Begin.Inventory + Net Purchase - Ending Inventory
31.500 = 10.000 + Net Purchase - 5.000
31.500 = Net Purchase + 5.000
Net Purchase = 31.500 - 5.000
Net Purchase = 26.500

6. Net Sales 67.000
COGS 11.000
Operating Expense 50.000
Tax Expense 1.000
Net Income After Tax?
Net Income After Tax = Net Sales - COGS - Operating Expense - Tax Expense
Net Income After Tax = 67.000 - 11.000 - 50.000 - 1.000
Net Income After Tax = 5.000

7. Sales 120.000
Sales Return 3.000
Sales Discount 1.500
Freight in 700
Freight out 800
Net Sales?
Net Sales = Sales - Sales Return - Sales Discount
Net Sales = 120.000 - 3.000 - 1.500
Net Sales = 115.500

18. terdapat data Pt ananda teknik sbg berikut: purchase Rp.246.000.000 purchase return Rp. 44.000.000 Freight collected Rp.5.000.000 inventory of 1 des 2015 Rp. 420.000.000 inventory of 31 des 2015 Rp. 240.000.000 sales Rp. 800.000.000 sales return: Rp. 44.000.000. maka gross profitnya adalah


Net purchase
= purchase - purchase return + freight collected
= 246.000.000 - 44.000.000 + 5.000.000
= 207.000.000

Net sales
= sales - sales return
= 800.000.000 - 44.000.000
= 756.000.000

COGS
= inventory of 1 dec + net purchase - inventory of 31 dec
= 420.000.000 + 207.000.000 - 240.000.000
= 387.000.000

Gross profit
= net sales - COGS
= 756.000.000 - 387.000.000
= 369.000.000

Jawaban:

To calculate the missing values, let's analyze each item step by step:

(1) Beginning Merchandise Inventory

We already have the value: Rp 7,000,000.00

(2) Ending Merchandise Inventory

We already have the value: Rp 6,000,000.00

(3) Purchase

We have the Net Purchase, but to find the Gross Purchase, we need to consider the Purchase Returns and Allowances and Purchase Discounts:

Gross Purchase = Net Purchase + Purchase Returns and Allowances - Purchase Discounts

Gross Purchase = Rp 25,000,000.00 + Rp 3,500,000.00 - Rp 1,000,000.00

Gross Purchase = Rp 27,500,000.00

(4) Sales

To find the Net Sales, we need to consider the Sales Returns and Allowances and Sales Discounts:

Net Sales = Gross Sales - Sales Returns and Allowances - Sales Discounts

Net Sales = Rp 34,000,000.00 - Rp 4,500,000.00 - Rp 1,200,000.00

Net Sales = Rp 28,300,000.00

(5) Purchase Returns

We already have the value: Rp 3,500,000.00

Now let's calculate the missing values:

(a) Sales Returns and Allowances

Sales Returns and Allowances = Net Sales - Gross Sales + Sales Discounts

Sales Returns and Allowances = Rp 28,300,000.00 - Rp 34,000,000.00 + Rp 1,200,000.00

Sales Returns and Allowances = Rp -4,500,000.00 + Rp 1,200,000.00

Sales Returns and Allowances = Rp -3,300,000.00

(b) Gross Sales

Gross Sales = Net Sales + Sales Returns and Allowances + Sales Discounts

Gross Sales = Rp 28,300,000.00 - Rp 3,300,000.00 + Rp 1,200,000.00

Gross Sales = Rp 26,200,000.00

Please note that the negative value for Sales Returns and Allowances indicates a credit transaction or returns made by customers.

(1) Beginning Merchandise Inventory: Rp 7,000,000.00

(2) Ending Merchandise Inventory: Rp 6,000,000.00

(4) Sales: Rp 34,000,000.00

Setiap bulannya, pendapatan sales tersebut dari penjualan laptop dinyatakan dalam fungsi f(x) = 1.200.000 + 0,07x, sedangkan pendapatan dari penjualan smartphone dinyatakan dalam fungsi g(x) = 1.000.000 + 0,05x, . Selisih pendapatan sales dari penjualan kedua barang tersebut adalah


Jawab:

200.000 + 0,02x

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

selisih, misalkan dilambangkan dengan s(x), dapat dicari dengan mengurangkan kedua fungsi:

s(x) = f(x) - g (x)

s(x) = (1.200.000+0,07x) - (1.000.000+0,05x)

s(x) = 1.200.000 + 0,07x - 1.000.000 - 0,05x

s(x) = 200.000 + 0,02x
