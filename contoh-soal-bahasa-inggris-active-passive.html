7. tolong kakak tugas bahasa Inggris tentang passive dan active voice, ​


Jawaban:

1. Simple Present

1.a) The nurse helps me.

I am helped by the nurse.

1.b) The nurse helps Jane.

Jane is helped by the nurse.

1.c) The nurse helps us.

We are helped by the nurse.

2. Simple Past

2.a) The nurse helped me.

I was helped by the nurse.

2.b) The nurse helped them.

They were helped by the nurse.

3. Present Perfect

3.a) The nurse has helped Joe.

Joe has been helped by the nurse.

3.b) The nurse has helped us.

We have been helped by the nurse.

4. Future

4.a) The nurse will help us.

We will be helped by the nurse.

4.b) The nurse is going to help Tim.

Tim is going to be helped by the nurse.

5.a) Mr. hopkins invited m to dinner.

I was invited to dinner by Mr. Hopkins.

5.b) Edison invented the phonograph.

Phonograph was invented by Edison.

6.a) A plumber is going to fix the leaky faucet.

The leaky faucet was going to be fixed by a plumber.

6.b) A nurse has examined the sick child.

The sick child has been examined by a nurse.

7.a) Shakespeare wrote Hamlet.

Hamlet was written by Shakespeare.

7.b) This news will amaze him.

He will be amazed by this news.

8. contoh soal active passive dan jawabannya


Contoh Soal

Change the active to the Passive!

She buy a book.Riko always eats a piece of cake before going to work.I do homework.She gives me a mobile phone.He wears T-shirt.They play dolls.Neni brings some books.I read a Magazine Kartini.Dani fixs The computers.I give Rima the pen.

Answer :

A book is bought by her.A piece of cake is always eaten by Riko.Homework is done by me.I am given a mobile phone by her.T-shirt is wore by him.Dolls are played by them.Some books are brought by Neni.A magazine Kartini is read by me.The computers are fixed by Dani.Rima is given the pen by me.

Pembahasan

Kalimat Pasif [Passive Voice] merupakan kalimat dimana subjek dikenai oleh pekerjaan. Kalimat pasif selalu mempunyai rumus be + Verb3. Subjek dikalimat aktif akan menjadi objek dikalimat pasif. Begitu pula, objek dikalimat aktif akan menjadi subjek dikalimat pasif. Kalimat diatas menggunakan simple present tense. Jadi Rumus yang diguanakan adalah :

Active Voice : Subject + Verb1 (s/es) + Objek

Passive Voice : Subjek + Am/Is/Are + Verb 3 + by + Objek

Keterangan :

Am digunakan jika subjek berupa I.Is diguanakan jika subjek berupa she, he, it (singular noun).Are digunakan jika subjek berupa you, we, they, plural noun.

Contoh :

She buy a book. => A book is bought by her.I do homework. => Homework is done by me.They play dolls. => Dolls are played by them.Neni brings some books. => Some books are brought by Neni.

9. kalimat future tense active dan passive menggunakan kata invite dalam bahasa Inggris​


1.tense acitive:

1Kami akan mengundang mereka ke pesta besok.(We will invite them to the party tomorrow)

2.Dia akan mengundang teman-temannya untuk nonton film malam minggu depan. (He will invite his friends over to see a movie next week.)

3.Mereka berencana mengundangnya ke pernikahan mereka pada bulan Juli.(They invited him to their wedding in July.)

2.Passive Tense:

1.Mereka akan diundang ke konferensi bulan depan. (They will be invited to the conference next month.)

2.Karyawan akan diundang ke perayaan ulang tahun perusahaan.(Employees will be invited to the company anniversary celebration.)

3.Dia akan diundang untuk berbicara di seminar minggu depan. (He will be invited to speak at the seminar next week.)

PENJELASAN:

Perlu diingat bahwa dalam kalimat passive, subjek menerima tindakan (diundang), sedangkan dalam kalimat active, subjek melakukan tindakan (mengundang).

31. contoh soal bahasa inggris bab passive voice + jawabannya


We use passive voice when the subject receives the action of the verb. When we know who the subject is, we put it at the end with by. We call this an agent.

Pembahasan

Contoh soal bahasa Inggris bab passive voice dan jawabannya yaitu

1. This science project can ... done by us.

The correct word to complete the sentence is ...

A. Been

B. Being

C. Be

D. Bees

Answer: Be (C).

Pada passive voice, kita harus memperhatikan ciri-ciri kalimat yang menunjukkan jenis tense yang digunakan.

Kata kunci pada kalimat di atas adalah kata "can", yang menunjukkan bahwa kalimat tersebut adalah modals.

Rumus passive modals adalah object + can + be + verb 3.

B. What did cooked by my mother yesterday?

C. What is cooked by my mother yesterday?

D. What were cooked by my mother yesterday?

Answer: What was cooked by my mother yesterday? (A)

Pembahasan:

Passive voice adalah suatu bentuk kalimat dimana subjek kalimat menerima aksi, bukan melakukan aksi. Tidak seperti active voice yang fokus terhadap pihak yang melakukan aksi (doer of action), bentuk ini lebih berfokus kepada pihak atau objek yang menerima hasil dari suatu aksi tersebut (receiver of action).

Voice yang fokus terhadap pihak yang melakukan aksi (doer of action), bentuk ini lebih berfokus kepada pihak atau objek yang menerima hasil dari suatu aksi tersebut (receiver of action).

1.Active = My parents plant some flowers

(Pelaku aksi)

2.Passive = Some flowers are planted by my parents

(Penerima aksi)

Umumnya, kalimat aktif yang bisa ditransformasi menjadi kalimat pasif berbentuk kalimat aktif yang mengandung transitive verbs (kata kerja yang memerlukan objek langsung) seperti make, bring, buy, write dan sebagainya. Sebaliknya, kalimat pasif yang mengandung intransitive verbs (kata kerja yang tidak memerlukan objek langsung) seperti cry, swim, go, arrive, dan die tidak bisa diubah menjadi kalimat pasif.

Namun, apabila intransitive verbs tersebut diikuti oleh preposition, ada kemungkinan kalimat tersebut bisa diubah menjadi kalimat pasif tetapi kalimat pasif yang terbentuk akan sangat kaku.

Rumus Passive Voice

Untuk membentuk kalimat pasif, rumus sederhana berikut biasanya digunakan tetapi tetap harus disesuaikan dengan tense kalimat.

Dan Rumusnya terlampir pada gambar di atas

Penggunaan kalimat pasif dengan perfect continuous tenses biasanya jarang dilakukan karena menghasilkan struktur kalimat yang terlalu rumit dan sulit dipahami. Oleh karena itu, hal ini sebaiknya dihindari meskipun secara tata bahasa benar (grammatically correct).

40. rumus active passive dan contohnya..........?


Cara mudah rumus passive adl to be + v3. Contoh simplr present tense = o + am/is/are + v3. Present continuous = o + am/is are+ being + v3. (Jd, kl present, to be-nya am/is/are). Simple past tense = o + was/were + v3. Past continuous + o + was/were + being + v3 (jadi, pada past, to be-nya was/were). Nah pd continous, ada tambahan being. Pd perfect, aux verb + been + v3. Contoh present perfect = o+ have/has+ been +v3. Pd past perfect = h O+ had been + v3. Pada future dan modals= o+ will/modals+ be + v3
