1. Contoh Asking for Repetition


Answer:

People usually ask for repetition due to the fact that they are not sure, or don't understand the point of the other party's talk. It could also be said because the other party doesn't talk clear or loud enough. Here's the example of asking for repetition.

- can you repeat it please?

- could you say that again?

- say that again?

- come again?

- could you possibly repeat one more time, please?

Kelas: SMP

Mapel: English

Kategori: Questions for repetition

38. Contoh dialog asking for repetition 2 orang


Asking for repetition adalah salah satu ungkapan (expression) yang sering digunakan dalam dialog sehari-hari tentang berbagai hal. Misalnya ketika kita kurang jelas pada saat mendengarkan lawan bicara, lalu memintanya untuk mengulangi lagi apa yang diucapkannya. Ini diperlukan agar informasi yang kita terima jelas dan tidak salah tafsir.

Beberapa ungkapan untuk meminta pengulangan (asking for repetition):

What? (apa?)

Could you repeat that? (Bisakah kamu mengulanginya?)

Can you say that once again? (bisakah mengatakannya sekali lagi?)

Sorry, I can't hear what you said. (Maaf, aku tidak mendengar yang kau ucapkan).

I don't quite understand what you've just said, would you mind repeating it again? (Saya tidak begitu mengerti yang Anda katakan barusan, tolong diulangi lagi).

Contoh dialog untuk 2 orang yang mengandung asking for repetition:

Tony: Hi Steve, where have you been? I haven't seen you for a week. (Hai Steve, dari mana saja kamu? Sudah seminggu aku tidak melihatmu).

Steve: I traveled to Wakanda and just went home yesterday. I have some business to do there. (Dari perjalanan ke Wakanda, dan aku baru pulang kemarin. Ada urusan yang harus dilakukan di sana).

Tony: Did you just say travel to Anaconda? Sorry, I didn't quite follow what you've just said. (Kamu bilang perjalanan ke Anakonda? Maaf, aku kurang paham yang kamu katakan barusan).

Steve: It's Wakanda, Tony, not Anaconda. Wakanda is a small but advanced country in Africa. (Wakanda, Tony, bukan Anaconda. Wakanda adalah negara kecil tapi canggih di benua Afrika).

Tony: Oh, I see now. (Oh, aku mengerti sekarang).
