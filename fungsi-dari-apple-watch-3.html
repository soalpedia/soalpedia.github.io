14. Marianne, Daisy and Annabella competed with each other in an apple pie food challenge. At the end of the challenge, Daisy ate 3 apple pies. Annabella ate 3 times as many apple pies as Daisy. Marianne ate 2/3 the number of apple pies that Annabella ate. How many apple pies did they eat altogether?


Let's start by finding out the number of apple pies each person ate:

Daisy ate 3 apple pies.

Annabella ate 3 times as many apple pies as Daisy, which is 3 * 3 = 9 apple pies.

Marianne ate 2/3 the number of apple pies that Annabella ate, which is (2/3) * 9 = 6 apple pies.

To find the total number of apple pies they ate altogether, we can add up their individual amounts:

3 + 9 + 6 = 18

Therefore, they ate a total of 18 apple pies altogether.

32. Jika membeli 3 apple dan 2 jeruk harganya 7.800 dan jika memebeli 2 apple dan 3 jeruk harganya 8.200 berapa harga 1 apel ditambah 1 jeruk?


itu yaa..semoga membantuu :)Diketahui :
- 3 apel + 2 jeruk = 7800
- 2 apel + 3 jeruk = 8200
- apel (a) dan jeruk (b)

Pembahasan :
Yang ditanyakan harga 1 apel dan 1 jeruk. Untuk mencari nya kita gunakan metode eliminasi dan metode substitusi.

Metode Eliminasi
3a + 2b = 7800 -- [dikalikan 2]
2a + 3b = 8200 -- [dikalikan 3]

6a + 4b = 15600
6a + 9b = 24600
-------------------------- --
     -5b = -9000
         b = 1800
                            
                            
Substitusikan
3a + 2b = 7800
3a + 2( 1800 ) = 7800
3a = 7800 - 3600
3a = 4200
a = 1400

Sehingga :
1 apel + 1 jeruk = a + b
1 apel + 1 jeruk = 1400 + 1800
1 apel + 1 jeruk = 3200

Jadi, harga 1 apel ditambah 1 jeruk jika harga 3 apel + 2 jeruk 7800 dan 2 apel + 3 jeruk 8200 adalah Rp3.200,00.
