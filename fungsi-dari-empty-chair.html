20. Compare the price of the following chair's - My Chair Rp. 1.500.000 - Santi's Chair Rp. 1.500.000 - Handoko's Chair Rp. 2.750.000 - Our Manager's Chair Rp. 5.000.000


Materi : Adjective

Spesifikasi : Comparison Degree

Kelas : -

- My Chair Rp. 1.500.000

- Santi's Chair Rp. 1.500.000

- Handoko's Chair Rp. 2.750.000

- Our Manager's Chair Rp. 5.000.000

Comparison :

=> My chair is as cheap as Santi's chair, but my chair is cheaper than Handoko's and our manager's chair.

=> Santi's chair is as cheap as my chair, but Santi's chair is cheaper than Handoko's and our manager's chair.

==> Handoko's chair is more expensive than Santi's and my chair, but Handoko's chair is cheaper than our manager's chair.

==> Our manager's chair is the most expensive of all.

37. Apa yang dimaksud empty?​


Empty merupakan kata sifat (adjective) yang apabila diterjemahkan berarti 'kosong' atau 'tidak berisi'.

Lawan kata dari empty adalah full yang berarti penuh.

Contoh kalimat :

"My glass is empty".

Artinya adalah "gelasku kosong" yang memiliki makna bahwa gelas tersebut kosong atau tidak ada isinya.
