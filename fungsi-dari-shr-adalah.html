30. Ubah ke passive voice 1.Daniel asked a qustion to mahendra 2.The worker built the house 3.they have given me a useful advice 4.shr will buy my phone 5.the teacher is telling us a joke


║ACTIVE-PASSIVE VOICE║

Answer

1. A question was asked to Mahendra by Daniel

or

Mahendra was asked a question by Daniel

2. The house was built by the worker.

3. A useful advice has been given to me by them

or

I have been given a useful advice by them

4. My phone will be bought by her.

5. A joke is being told to us by the teacher

or

We are being told a joke by the teacher.

Discussion

PASSIVE VOICEPassive voice is focused on the object and the action rather than the doer of the action. When we want to change the active voice to the passive voice, keep in mind that we must put the object at the beginning of sentence as well as the action. Meanwhile, the doer of the action will be the agent in the passive voice.Harap diperhatikan baik-baik, bahwa tidak semua kata kerja yang terlampir dalam kalimat aktif bisa dirubah ke dalam bentuk Passive. Pastikan bahwa kalimat aktif tersebut termasuk transitive verb ( dimana kata kerjanya memerlukan obyek untuk memperjelas informasi ). Passive voice tidak bisa merubah kata kerja tanpa obyek ( Intransitive verb ).  Contoh : Go, sleep, occur, happen .Dalam perubahan dari kalimat aktif ke passive, obyek kalimat aktif akan diposisikan sebagai Subyek dalam kalimat pasif. Sedangkan Subyek kalimat aktif akan diposisikan sebagai obyek preposisi (Agent ). Passive voice mengikuti bentuk tenses dan tidak merubah arti sebenarnya dari kalimat aktif.Harap diperhatikan pronoun nya ( kata ganti orang ). Pronoun akan berubah berdasarkan posisi kelas kata ( parts of speech ) dalam kalimat pasif.Ciri khas penerapan kalimat passive BE + VERB 3 ( past participle )

1. Rumus passive simple past  dengan dua obyek ( DO & IO )

S+Verb 2+ O/C    ( Active )

Daniel asked a question to mahendra

S(O)+tobe(was/were)+verb 3 + by + Agent   ( Passive )

A question was asked to Mahendra by Daniel

or

Mahendra was asked a question by Daniel

Terdapat 2 object yaitu direct object ( benda / noun yang langsung dikenai tindakan ) "A question." Sedangkan, Indirect object ( orang yang menerima direct object/ orang yang dituju dari dampak tindakan aksi  ) " Mahendra"

2. Rumus passive simple past

S+Verb 2 + O/C   ( Active )

The worker built the house

S(O)+tobe(was/were)+Verb 3 + by + Agent  ( Passive )

The house was built by the worker.

3. Rumus passive present perfect tense dengan dua obyek  ( DO & IO )

S+has/have+verb 3 + O/C    (Active )

they have given me a useful advice

S(O)+has/have+been + Verb 3 + by + Agent  ( Passive )

A useful advice has been given to me by them

or

I have been given a useful advice by them.

Terdapat 2 object yaitu direct object ( benda / noun yang langsung dikenai tindakan ) "A useful advice." Sedangkan, Indirect object ( orang yang menerima direct object/ orang yang dituju dari dampak tindakan aksi  ) " me"

4. Rumus passive simple future tense

S+will+verb 1 + O / C    ( Active )

she will buy my phone

S(O)+will+be+verb 3 + by + Agent  ( Passive )

My phone will be bought by her.

5. Rumus passive present continuous tense dengan 2 obyek ( DO & IO )

S+tobe(is/am/are)+verb ing + O/C    ( Active )

the teacher is telling us a joke.

S(O)+tobe(is/am/are)+being+verb 3+by +Agent  ( Passive )

A joke is being told to us by the teacher

or

We are being told a joke by the teacher.

Terdapat 2 object yaitu direct object ( benda / noun yang langsung dikenai tindakan ) "A joke." Sedangkan, Indirect object ( orang yang menerima direct object/ orang yang dituju dari dampak tindakan aksi  ) " us"
