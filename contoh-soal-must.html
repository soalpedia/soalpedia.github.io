1. contoh soal (+)(-) dari auxiliary verb must​


Jawaban:

Positive form:

1. You must complete your homework before you can go out to play.

2. She must arrive on time for the important meeting.

3. I must remember to buy groceries on my way home.

4. They must study hard to pass their exams.

5. We must follow the safety guidelines to prevent accidents.

Negative form:

1. You must not ignore your responsibilities at work.

2. She must not forget to lock the door when she leaves.

3. I must not waste my time on unproductive activities.

4. They must not break the rules during the game.

5. We must not underestimate the importance of good communication in a team.

18. tuliskan contoh 5 soal menggunakan should, might,could, must


Should : harus/seharusnya
~ I should go to bed right now.
~She should lose weight.

Might : mungkin
~ I might have done something wrong.
~ She might want to hang out with us.

Could : bisa/mungkin
~ Her friend could speak five different languages.
~ It might be possible to do this elsewhere.

Must : harus (lebih memaksa dari should)
~ You must obey the rules in the office.
~ Every worker must complete his/her assignment by next Monday.

40. Contoh penggunaan kata "must" dan "must not" di rumah sakit dalam bahasa Inggris


Patients must follow the instructions given by their doctors.
(Artinya: Pasien harus mengikuti instruksi yang diberikan oleh dokter mereka.)

Visitors must wear a mask when entering the hospital.
(Artinya: Pengunjung harus memakai masker ketika masuk ke rumah sakit.)

Doctors must not disclose confidential patient information.
(Artinya: Dokter tidak boleh mengungkapkan informasi rahasia pasien.)

Nurses must wash their hands before and after treating each patient.
(Artinya: Perawat harus mencuci tangan sebelum dan setelah merawat setiap pasien.)

Patients must not smoke in the hospital premises.
(Artinya: Pasien tidak boleh merokok di area rumah sakit.)

Hospital staff must follow safety protocols to prevent the spread of infectious diseases.
(Artinya: Staf rumah sakit harus mengikuti protokol keamanan untuk mencegah penyebaran penyakit menular.)

Visitors must not disturb other patients or interfere with medical procedures.
(Artinya: Pengunjung tidak boleh mengganggu pasien lain atau mengganggu prosedur medis.)
